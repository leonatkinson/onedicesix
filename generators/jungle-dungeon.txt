    The entrance is {entrance} that opens to room 1's {door-direction} side.<br>
    <br>
    Passages are not necessarily straight. Longer passages may make several
    turns, especially to connect various sides of rooms. Passages may
    also cross or overlap. Doorways are located approximately by a side
    of a room (e.g. northern), leaving exact placement up to iterpretation.
    There may be multiple exits from a room going in the same direction.
    In all cases of ambiguity, the map artist may make an arbitrary
    decision to conform to the given parameters. The freedom includes
    the choice to move passages in the vertical dimension with stairs
    and slopes.
    <br>
    <br>
    {rooms}
