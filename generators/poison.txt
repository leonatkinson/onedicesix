    {permanence}.

11-12   induces sleep for {sleep-duration}
13-14   reduces {stat} by {stat-damage}, {damage-save}. {damage-repeat}

17-18   causes confusion, save for no effect
19      causes memory loss, save vs poison or lose 1d6 memorized spells
